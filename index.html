The Academic Rollercoaster

They say college is the best four years of your life. They conveniently leave out the part about the 3 AM library breakdowns, the existential dread, and surviving entirely on iced lattes and dining hall pizza. But walking across that stage to grab my B.A. in Communications? Totally worth every single all-nighter.
